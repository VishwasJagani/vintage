Featured Cars

Chevrolet Chevelle SS

454 cubic inches V8, 450hp, back in the 70s, was an insane amount of power. See the pure American MOPAR goodness in this gorgeously preserved Chevy Chevelle SS, from the goodies of the SS package to the deafening sound of the big-block V8.

Porsche 356A Speedster

Feel the true roots of the legendary automative brand Porsche, with the 356A Speedster. Made in the 1950s, this car features a timeless design that shaped Porsche for years to come. Experience this 356A in all its glory at Vintage Autohaus.

Mercedes-Benz 190SL

One of the most beautiful cars ever made, the Mercedes-Benz 190SL is the queen of the 1950s. It is one of the rarest, most sought-after Mercedes models. Experience one in pristine condition with an optional ride-in package today.

Shelby AC Cobra 427

One of the rarest cars on the floor, the Shelby Cobra 427 was the pride and joy of the legendary racer Carrol Shelby. A 7-liter V8 and lightweight chassis propel this car from 0-60 in 3.4 seconds, that makes it the fastest cars of its time.

Audi S1 Sport Quattro

Picture this: It's 1985. You're Audi. You want to win Group-B Rally. What do you do? You make a 4WD, 500HP monster. This is the Audi S1. This car shaped Audi for the future, with the remarkable Quattro drive system. A piece of history, right here, at the Vintage Autohaus.

Ferrari Dino 246 GT

One of the very rare Ferraris with a V6, the Dino 246 GT featured groundbreaking design. It is the first "mass-produced" Ferraris, with a distinct design and driving experience, with the synchronized 5-speed manual transmission. Seeing is believing with this one, for sure.
